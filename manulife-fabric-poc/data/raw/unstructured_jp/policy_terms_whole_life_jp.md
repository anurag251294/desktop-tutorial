# Whole Life Insurance with Disability Rider — Policy Terms (Manulife Japan)

**Effective**: 2025-04-01
**Jurisdiction**: Japan (Financial Services Agency)
**Currency**: Japanese Yen (JPY)

## 1. Eligibility

- Issue age: 18 to 70 years
- Resident of Japan with valid residency status
- Pre-policy medical questionnaire required for coverage above JPY 50,000,000
- Full medical examination required for coverage above JPY 100,000,000

## 2. Premium Payment

- Premium frequencies: Monthly, Quarterly, Semi-Annual, Annual
- Accepted payment methods: bank transfer (furikomi), direct debit (kouza furikae), convenience store (konbini) payments
- Grace period: 60 days from premium due date before policy lapse
- Yen denominated; no foreign-currency rider on this product

## 3. Contestability Period

The policy is contestable for the first **24 months** from the effective date. During this period, the insurer may rescind the policy for material misrepresentation in the application or medical questionnaire. After 24 months, the policy becomes incontestable except for cases of fraud.

## 4. Suicide Exclusion

Death benefit excluded for deaths by suicide within the first **24 months** from the effective date. Premiums paid will be refunded to the beneficiary. After 24 months, the suicide exclusion no longer applies.

## 5. Disability Rider

The Disability Rider waives all future premiums if the insured becomes totally and permanently disabled before age 60 and the disability persists continuously for **180 days**. Disability must be certified by a licensed physician registered with the Japan Medical Association.

## 6. Cash Value & Policy Loans

- Cash value accrues from the third policy anniversary
- Policy loan available up to 90% of cash value at the prevailing rate set by the FSA-published rate schedule
- Outstanding loans reduce the death benefit

## 7. Surrender

The policyholder may surrender the policy at any time. Surrender value is published in the policy schedule. Surrender within the first 24 months results in zero cash value due to acquisition cost recovery.

## 8. Beneficiary Designation

Up to four primary beneficiaries with percentage allocations summing to 100%. Beneficiary changes require a signed amendment form filed at any Manulife Japan branch office.

## 9. Claim Process

- Notification: within 30 days of the insured event
- Documentation: death certificate (shibo shoumeisho) from the municipal office, original policy, beneficiary identification
- Standard settlement: 5 business days from completion of documentation
- Disputed claims: forwarded to the Claims Review Committee with 30-day SLA
