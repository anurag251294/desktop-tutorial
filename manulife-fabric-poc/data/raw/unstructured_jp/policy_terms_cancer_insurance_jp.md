# Cancer Insurance Plus — Policy Terms (Manulife Japan)

**Effective**: 2025-04-01

## 1. Coverage Scope

Lump-sum benefit on first diagnosis of malignant neoplasm covered under ICD-10 codes C00 through C97 (excluding C44 non-melanoma skin cancer, which receives 10% of the face value).

- Hospitalisation benefit: JPY 10,000 per day, up to 730 days per disease
- Outpatient cancer treatment benefit: JPY 5,000 per visit, up to 30 visits per year
- Advanced medical treatment rider: covers up to JPY 20,000,000 lifetime for designated advanced therapies as classified by Japan's Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare

## 2. Waiting Period

A **90-day waiting period** applies from the effective date. Cancers diagnosed within the waiting period are excluded and result in a full premium refund.

## 3. Pre-Existing Conditions

Pre-existing cancers or in-situ neoplasms disclosed in the application are permanently excluded. Undisclosed pre-existing conditions discovered during a claim investigation will result in claim denial and possible policy rescission within the 24-month contestability period.

## 4. Hospital Stay Definition

For the hospitalisation benefit:
- Minimum 1 night admission to a hospital registered with Japan's medical insurance system
- Day surgery and same-day discharge are covered under the outpatient benefit, not hospitalisation
- ICU stays are reimbursed at 1.5x the daily rate

## 5. Second Opinion Service

The insured may request a second medical opinion service at no additional cost. Manulife Japan coordinates with one of 18 designated tertiary cancer centres including the National Cancer Center Tokyo, Osaka International Cancer Institute, and Kyushu Cancer Center.

## 6. Premium Adjustment

Premiums are level for the duration of the policy. No medical re-underwriting for ongoing claims.

## 7. Exclusions

The following are not covered:
- Cancer diagnosed within the 90-day waiting period
- Self-inflicted illness or injury
- Cancer caused by nuclear radiation outside of authorised medical treatment
- Treatments not authorised by Japan's MHLW
