# Manulife Japan — Annual Highlights 2025

## Strategic Themes

2025 was the second year of Manulife Japan's "Customer Connect 2027" strategy with three pillars:
1. Digital-first customer service (My Manulife Japan portal MAU exceeded 1.2M)
2. Variable annuity leadership in the wealth transfer segment
3. Distribution network expansion into Hokkaido and Kyushu

## Financial Performance

- New business APE (annualised premium equivalent): JPY 187.4B, +6.2% year-over-year
- Total premium income: JPY 1.42T
- AUM at year-end: JPY 7.8T (+11% YoY driven by net inflows and market performance)
- Net income (IFRS 17 basis): JPY 134.6B, +4.1% YoY
- Contractual service margin (CSM): JPY 2.61T, +5.4% YoY

## Product Mix

| Line | Share of new business APE |
|------|---------------------------|
| Wealth (Variable Annuities, Single-Premium Whole Life) | 48% |
| Life Insurance (Term, Whole Life, Endowment) | 32% |
| Health (Cancer, Critical Illness, Medical) | 17% |
| Group Pension | 3% |

## Distribution Footprint

- Branch offices: 47 (+3 from 2024 — added Hakodate, Kumamoto, and Kanazawa)
- Active advisors: 1,847 (+8% YoY)
- Tied bancassurance partnerships: 22 (renewed Mizuho and SMBC agreements; signed new Hokkaido regional bank)
- Independent broker network: 280+ firms

## Customer Service

- Net Promoter Score: +34 (industry leading among life insurers in Japan)
- Average claim settlement time: 6.2 business days
- Complaint volume: 0.12% of policies (declined from 0.15% in 2024)

## Regulatory & Risk

- Full IFRS 17 reporting first year
- Solvency margin ratio: 824% (regulatory minimum 200%)
- Cyber resilience: completed FSA tabletop exercise; zero customer-data incidents
- AML alerts processed: 1,142 (89% closed within SLA)

## Outlook 2026

Continued focus on:
- Variable annuity demand from the demographic transition (rising 65+ population)
- Group benefits sales tied to corporate wage-cycle reforms
- Investment in data and AI platform — including a unified Microsoft Fabric foundation to consolidate the current ADF, Databricks, and Cosmos DB landscape
