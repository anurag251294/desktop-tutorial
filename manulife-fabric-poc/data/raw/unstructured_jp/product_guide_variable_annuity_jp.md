# Variable Annuity Plus — Product Guide (Manulife Japan)

**Product Line**: Wealth Management
**Currency**: JPY
**Target Customer**: Retail and Mass Affluent, age 30-75

## 1. Product Concept

A unit-linked variable annuity offering a guaranteed minimum death benefit, optional guaranteed minimum withdrawal benefit (GMWB), and access to a curated platform of 10+ Manulife mutual funds spanning Japanese equities, Asia Pacific, US technology, and Japanese government bonds.

## 3. Fees

- Annual management charge: 1.30% of NAV
- Mortality & expense (M&E) charge: 0.85% of NAV
- Fund-of-funds expense: 0.20% - 0.95% depending on fund mix
- Surrender charge: 7% in year 1, declining 1% per year for 7 years, zero thereafter

## 4. Income Phase

Annuitisation may begin at any policy anniversary on or after age 60. Income options:
- Lifetime income (single or joint)
- Period certain (10 / 15 / 20 years)
- Lump sum (subject to surrender charges if before year 8)

## 5. Tax Treatment in Japan

- Premiums are eligible for the personal annuity insurance premium deduction (kojin nenkin hoken-ryou koujo)
- Annuity payouts: portion attributable to interest is taxed as miscellaneous income
- Death benefits: subject to inheritance tax with the standard JPY 5,000,000 per legal heir exemption

## 6. Suitability

This product is **not suitable** for:
- Customers requiring liquidity within the first 7 years
- Customers with capital-preservation as the sole objective (better served by yen money market)
- Customers under age 30 with a >35 year horizon (a pure-equity unit-trust is typically lower cost)
