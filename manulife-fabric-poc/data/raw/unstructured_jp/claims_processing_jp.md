# Claims Processing Guidelines — Manulife Japan

**Audience**: Claims adjusters, branch managers, customer service representatives
**Version**: 2026.Q1

## 1. Service Standards

| Claim Type | Acknowledgement | First Decision | Final Settlement |
|------------|----------------|----------------|------------------|
| Hospitalisation (daily benefit) | 1 business day | 3 business days | 5 business days |
| Surgery benefit | 1 business day | 5 business days | 7 business days |
| Cancer (lump sum) | 1 business day | 7 business days | 10 business days |
| Death benefit | 1 business day | 10 business days | 14 business days |
| Disability rider | 1 business day | 14 business days | 21 business days |
| Annuity withdrawal | Same day | 2 business days | 3 business days |

All timelines exclude documents pending from the policyholder.

## 2. Documentation Requirements

**All claims:**
- Original or certified copy of policy contract
- Government-issued photo ID (driver's licence, My Number card, residence card)
- Bank account confirmation for payout (passbook copy or bank statement)

**Death benefit additionally:**
- Death certificate (shibo shoumeisho) issued by municipal office
- Detailed medical certificate (shibo shindansho) from the attending physician
- Family register excerpt (koseki tohon) confirming beneficiary relationship

**Cancer / critical illness:**
- Pathology report from a Japan-licensed pathologist
- Treatment plan from oncology or specialist physician
- Hospital admission/discharge records (nyuintaiin shoumeisho)

## 3. Adjuster Authority Levels

- **Adjuster (junior)**: up to JPY 2,000,000 — straight-through approval, no second review
- **Senior adjuster**: up to JPY 10,000,000 — peer review required
- **Adjuster supervisor**: up to JPY 50,000,000 — Claims Review Committee fast track
- **Claims Director**: above JPY 50,000,000 — board-of-claims review

## 4. Suspicious Claims & AML

Claims flagged for further review when any of the following are present:
- Claim within 24 months of policy issue (contestability period)
- Claim amount within 10% of policy maximum coverage
- Claimant address change within 90 days prior to claim
- Same beneficiary on multiple recent claims
- Geographic mismatch between policy issue location and claim event location

Flagged claims are routed to the AML/Fraud Investigation Unit within 1 business day. Initial review must complete within 5 business days.

## 5. Denial Communication

Denial letters must include:
- Specific policy clause cited
- Documentation reviewed
- Right of appeal and 60-day window
- Contact for ombudsman / Japan Financial Services Agency consumer hotline
